Awards and Affiliations
There are no limits on what you can achieve with your life, except the limits you accept in your mind. ~ Brian Tracy.
The true measure of achievement is not just receiving awards or accolades, it is doing something that you appreciate, something you believe is worthwhile.
Harvard Crossroad Emerging Leader
HBS Club of the GCC
The Harvard Crossroads Emerging Leaders Program is a prestigious and a fully funded career development opportunity for students from across the world. Following a rigorous multi-stage evaluation process, exceptional students are selected from a diverse applicant pool to take part in this unique educational experience. Selected as one of the 80 participants out of over 6000 initial applicants to attend the fully funded in person Dubai program sponsored and powered by Harvard University.
YALI Cohort-11 Fellow
YALI RLC
YALI Regional Leadership Centers (RLCs) is a body of institution that offers leadership trainings to young African Leaders between the ages of 18-35.The RLCs are a project of the United States Agency for International Development (USAID) in close partnership with The MasterCard Foundation and other private sector partners. I was selected as one of the very few qualified participant from a pool of thousands of application, to be part of the Online Cohort 11. I was taken through various forms of leadership training and received extensive orientation on civil engagement.
V.C Scholar Award
Lagos State University
Recognized and lauded by the Vice Chancellor of the Lagos state University as one of the most outstanding student in the institution. Following several impacts and wins, I was awarded the Scholar award and recognized as duly representing the school on all fronts.
UN Millenium Campus Network Fellow
UN Millenium Campus Network
I was selected as one of the few young change makers who participated in the 2018 cohort of the Millennium Campus Network from a pool of thousands of applications. I worked alongside the two campus director and co-fellows to organize and lead meaninful conversations (project management, servant leadership) that are imperative to achieving the SDGs. Following the impact of the fellowship, i got featured as the spotlight for a month due to the impact of my work in the social development space and for representing the fellowship well.
YPB Fellow
Young Professional Bootcamp
The young professional Bootcamp is a program for high potential 20-25 year olds to learn world class practical training, inspire innovation, drive success in the marketplace and ultimately lead to transformation of the Nigerian economy.I was one of the 200 selected participants who attended the 5days residential bootcamp from a pool of over 2000 applications accross Nigeria. I participated in the cooperate career stream and gained mastery of strategic and principled building of a successful career in social innovation.
Youth In Development Fellow
SOZO Network
Sozo Network is a non-profit organization known for promoting the participation of young people in leadership and sustainable development. Based on exceptional performance during the 4-days bootcamp, I was selected as part of the 20 Youth in development shortlisted from over 200 participants, to participate in a 3 months professional development experience. I Received exclusive mentorship, strategy sessions and partnerships. I had access to development experts mentorship which strengthened and improved my organizational performance.
